Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the dieHaarbanduschi Ads Manager application ("App") accesses and uses data from Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram) on behalf of dieHaarbanduschi GmbH.

1. Who We Are

This App is operated by Lennart Duschek on behalf of dieHaarbanduschi GmbH. For privacy-related questions, please contact: mail@lennartduschek.de

2. What Data We Access

The App accesses the following data via the Meta API:

  • Ad Account data: Campaigns, ad sets, ads, creatives, insights (spend, impressions, clicks, ROAS)
  • Instagram data: Comments on business posts, profile information
  • Page data: Facebook Page engagement and post information

3. How We Use the Data

The data is used exclusively for internal business purposes:

  • Analyzing and optimizing Meta advertising campaigns
  • Reading and responding to Instagram comments
  • Storing aggregated ad performance data in an internal ClickHouse database

We do not sell, share, or distribute any data to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Data accessed via the Meta API is stored on a private, internal server operated by dieHaarbanduschi GmbH. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained for internal analytics purposes and deleted upon request or when no longer needed for business operations.

6. Third-Party Services

This App uses the Meta Graph API. Use of Meta platforms is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

If you have questions about data we hold or wish to request deletion, please contact us at mail@lennartduschek.de.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
